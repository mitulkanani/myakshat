Tailored Shirts and Tailored Suits, Simplified.

Tailored Suits and Tailored Shirts are for Everyone.

Your clothes are an expression of who you are, so they should account for the fact that your arms may be a little longer than most, or that you’re broad in the shoulders.

At Tailors Mark, we use technology to simplify the fitting and process for tailored suits and tailored shirts, which means you can be sized, fitted, and ready to order online in just a few minutes.

With Tailors Mark, you can start creating your own custom wardrobe online in just a few minutes. And because we’re careful through the tailoring process, we’ve become the most positively reviewed tailoring company on Google in Australia.

You can choose from thousands of tailored shirts and tailored suits styles and add custom details, all made by hand to your exact specifications, with fabrics and materials sourced from around the world.

When you buy your suit off the shelf, you’re buying a suit designed for someone else.

You’re an individual. Dress like one.

Happy Shopping.

Tailoring Specialists, Global Leaders in Bespoke Suits and Shirts.

At Tailors Mark, we’ve curated our range of materials and fabrics over years, always working with quality suppliers from around the world. We always try to narrow down the selection process when you’re shopping online, but we’ll always provide depth and detail when you’re selecting your materials and fabrics.

We cater to both every day ware and special occasions, with a selection of custom made suits and shirts for work,custom made shirts, wedding suits, grooms suits, and tailored chinos.

Once you’re fitted and online, we’ve created a really simple process that helps you to select your customisation. You can select from colours and fabrics, signature details and finishes, place your order, and receive your tailored clothing within three weeks.

Crafted Individuality.

At Tailors Mark, we realise that there’s a certain romance with buying custom clothing. The idea that you’re sized by individually, that someone then takes the materials and creates a tailored shirts or tailored suits to your exact specifications.

We’ve got unlimited combinations to choose from, with one of the largest catalogues online, so you can select your hand made materials with confidence, knowing that you won’t bump into someone in the same custom suit or custom shirt as you when you’re out at a party or at a wedding.

At the same time, with anything custom made there’s always a small margin for error. So it’s been our goal to always go above and beyond what is required to make sure you’re fully satisfied with the fit and process of buying a Tailors Mark suit, pants, or tailored shirts. It’s how we’ve developed our reputation in the luxury clothing and high-end custom clothing space.

Once we’ve got your fit, then you’ll never have to be measured again. So it’s really easy to order custom suits and custom shirts for special events, work, and weddings whenever you please.
