Important Imformation:

To make a purchase using afterpay, you need:

  • - A Visa or Mastercard credit/debit card
  • - To be over 18 years of age
  • - An Australian residential address

The payment arrangement is made with Afterpay and they will assess your order instantly. If Afterpay approves your order, new customers will pay their first instalment at the time of purchase, with payments over 6 weeks. When Afterpay knows you a little bit better, most returning Afterpay customers will pay their first instalment 14 days after purchase, with payments over 8 weeks from the time of purchase. The full payment schedule will be provided to you at time of purchase.

If you wish to return your goods, complete the online returns process as per the normal returns policy. If eligble for a refund, your initial payment will be returned in full and the remaining payments cancelled at no charge.

If you fail to make a payment, Afterpay will charge a late fee of $10, and if it's still overdue one week later, an additional $7 will be charged. These fees will be incurred for each payment that is late.

For more information about Afterpay terms and conditions, visit the Afterpay website.
