I. Do

No ordinary day,no ordinary grooms party.

Make a booking enquiry now
Photo Credit: Julia Lockhead Photography

At Tailors Mark, we know that your wedding day is an incredibly busy, expensive, and (most importantly) special time. That’s why we’ve simplified the entire process.

We’ll take the measurements for your entire grooms party in one fitting, help you choose suiting and shirting fabrics along with custom details, and have them hand tailored and delivered to your door well before your wedding day. Of course, any alterations or refinements to perfect each suit, is on us.

We use technology to simplify the tailoring process, which means that you can fully customise and tailor outfits for the whole grooms party, without spending any additional time or money in the process. With Tailors Mark, there’s one less thing to worry about, and one more thing to make your wedding day special.

Wedding Design Experts.

Custom wedding suits, shirts, and trousers, by the experts.

At Tailors Mark, all of our fitting staff and craftsmen are experienced in creating garments for wedding parties, so we’re schooled in the small details that really bring an ensemble to life.

When we’re dressing wedding parties, we lock down all of our fits first, and then work with you to choose colour, fabric, stitching, and button details. You’ll receive all of your garments in premium quality and custom design before your wedding day, and we’ll always leave a little extra time for those small touch ups at the last minute.

When it comes to professional elegant custom clothing, the fit and the quality are our first priority. That’s why we’ve sourced the best fabrics and materials from around the world - luxurious, hand made, custom fabrics that breathe and mould, woven to perfection so you feel like yourself on your wedding day.
